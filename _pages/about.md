Hi, I am Abderlrahman “Boda” Sadallah. I am a PhD student at [Università della Svizzera italiana (USI)](https://www.usi.ch/it), where I am supervised by [Prof. Lonneke van der Plas](https://sites.google.com/site/lonnekenlp/).  
Previously, I obtained my MSc from the NLP Department at [MBZUAI](https://mbzuai.ac.ae/), where I conducted my MSc research under the supervision of [Prof. Ted Briscoe](https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~ejb1/) and [Dr. Ekaterina Kochmar](https://ekochmar.github.io/about/).

My main research interests include:
- NLP applications for scientific documents and peer review  
- Arabic NLP, with a focus on cultural representation and evaluation in large language models


I serve as a reviewer at NLP conferences such as ACL and EMNLP.
