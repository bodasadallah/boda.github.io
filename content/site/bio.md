I am Boda Sadallah, a PhD student in NLP at [Università della Svizzera italiana (USI)](https://www.usi.ch/it), supervised by [Prof. Lonneke van der Plas](https://sites.google.com/site/lonnekenlp/).

I completed my MSc at [MBZUAI](https://mbzuai.ac.ae/) in the NLP department, where I worked under the supervision of [Prof. Ted Briscoe](https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~ejb1/) and [Dr. Ekaterina Kochmar](https://ekochmar.github.io/about/). I also collaborate with [Prof. Fajri Koto](https://fajrikoto.com/) and the [UKP Lab](https://www.ukp.tu-darmstadt.de/) led by [Prof. Iryna Gurevych](https://www.informatik.tu-darmstadt.de/ukp/ukp_home/head_ukp/index.en.jsp).

My research focuses on:
- **NLP for scientific documents** — peer review analysis, argument mining, document understanding
- **LLM evaluation** — cultural representation, bias, robustness, multilingual performance
- **Arabic NLP** — dialectal variation, low-resource settings, culturally-aware evaluation

More broadly, I'm interested in improving scientific publishing through better understanding and evaluation of research artifacts.

I regularly review for major NLP venues, including ACL and EMNLP.

Outside research, I enjoy running and mountain hiking.
